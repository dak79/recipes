Pizza Margherita

The most delicious pizza!

  • Difficulty: medium
  • Time: 30min + 2h and 30min for leavening

List of ingredients (for 2 pizzas):

Dough:
500g flour
300ml water
20g extra-virgin olive oil
10g salt
20g fresh brewer's yeast

For dress:
500g tomato pulp
400g mozzarella
1 small spoon oregano
1 small spoon salt
q.s. basil
q.s. extra-virgin olive oil

Steps:

First of all, let's prepare the dough.

  1. Put the water at room temperature in a jug and add the yeast.
  2. Mix it with a spoon until the yeast is completely dissolved.
  3. Put the flour in a big bowl and add, slowly, half of the water.
  4. Knead with hands and add the salt.
  5. Add the other half of water and keep on kneading.
  6. Keep on kneading until the dough is even.
  7. When the dough is even add, little by little, the oil and keep on kneading until the oil is absorbed.
  8. When the dough is smooth and elastic, let it rest for 10 minuts and after put it in a bowl.
  9. Cover the bowl with cling film and clean cloth and let leavening (in the dark) for at least 2 hours and 30 minuts in a temperature of 26°-28°.

The dress and cooking.

  1. Open the oven and let it reach 250°.
  2. Smash mozzarella to get rid of the water.
  3. Put tomato pulp in a bowl and add salt, olive oil and oregano.
  4. Take a baking sheet and grease it.
  5. Roll out the dough and put it in th baking sheet.
  6. Put the tomato pulp on the dough.
  7. Put the baking sheet in the oven for 7 minuts at 250°.
  8. Take out pizza, add mozzarella and put it in the oven for other 7 minuts at 250°.
  9. Take out pizza, put the basil on, and enjoy!

Spaghetti aglio, olio e peperoncino

Easy and perfect for summers!

  • Difficulty: easy
  • Time: 15min

List of ingredients (for 2 persons):
200g spaghetti
2 clove of garlic
q.s. extra-virgin olive oil
1 chili pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. When the water boil add and handful of rock salt.
  3. Meanwhile you wait the water to boil, prepare the sauce.
  4. Cut 2 cloves of garlic into small pieces.
  5. Cut chili pepper into small pieces
  6. Put the extra-virgin olive oil into a pan, the oil should cover the base of the pan.
  7. Put cutted garlic and chili pepper into oil.
  8. Open a slow fire under the pan
  9. When garlic start to get a light brown color close the fire.
  10. When the water boil put 200g of spaghetti inside it.
  11. After 9 minuts spaghetti are cooked, drain spaghetti.
  12. Add the souce.
  13. Enjoy!

Pappardelle al ragù

A winter pasta!

  • Difficulty: easy
  • Time: 10min + 2h and 30min for ragù sauce

List of ingredients (for 2 persons):
200g pappardelle
1 white onion
2 carrots
400g minced meat (veal)
half glass red wine
200g tomato pulp
q.s. salt
q.s. extra-virgin olive oil

Steps:

  1. Take a pot and put the extra-virgin olive oil in it.
  2. Cut the onion and the carrots in small pieces and put in the pot.
  3. Put a lid on the pot, open the fire and cook for 10 minuts.
  4. Add the minced meat, mix and add the half glass of red wine.
  5. When the wine is completly evaporate, add tomato pulp, mix and cover with lid.
  6. Let the souce cooking for 2 hour and half in very slow fire with lid (if it is needed add few water).
  7. When the souce is ready, boil a pot of water.
  8. When water boil add rock salt.
  9. When the salt dissolve, add 200g of pappardelle.
  10. In about 6 minuts pappardelle are cooked.
  11. Drain pappardelle and add the souce.
  12. Enjoy!
